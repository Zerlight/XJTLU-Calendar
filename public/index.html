Instructions

This tool automates the process of extracting your personal timetable from the E-Bridge system and converting it into a standard .ics calendar file that you can import into Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook.

  • The script will launch a new browser tab asking you to log into E-Bridge.
  • After logging in, do not close the browser! The script will automatically navigate the site to extract your Academic Calendar and Lesson Timetable.
  • Once complete, a download button will appear right here.
